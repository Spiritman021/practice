Railway Exam Arithmetic Plan

Railway arithmetic often tests speed in familiar patterns. Train your brain to spot these patterns immediately.

High-Frequency Skills

Tables 11 to 30, factor pairs, and quick division conversion carry strong return. Practice these daily in short bursts.

Timed Practice Model

Use 3 rounds: easy direct, moderate mixed, then full simulation. Compare accuracy across rounds to identify fatigue errors.

Error Reduction

Keep a one-page “repeat mistakes list” and revise before each mock. This prevents same error loops.
