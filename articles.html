Math Tools Strategy

Table Hunt, Squares, Cubes, Reverse Table, Consecutive Products: Use these tools for speed building. Keep one target: answer in under 3 seconds for direct table facts.

Best routine: 20 minutes daily. First 10 minutes revision (easy), next 10 minutes timed practice (mixed difficulty).

Mock Test Strategy

MCQ Mock + Multiplication Mock: Practice exam-like pressure. Attempt with timer, then spend equal time reviewing wrong answers.

For SSC/Bank style prep, keep negative-marking awareness and focus on accuracy before speed.

Focus & Concentration Strategy

Number Scan, Symbol Scan, Stroop Tap, Breathing, Dot Tracking, Meditation: These tools train visual attention, impulse control, and mental stability.

Use 8 to 12 minute focus drills before study blocks. This improves retention and reduces careless mistakes in quantitative sections.
