5-4-3-2-1 Grounding Focus

This activity helps you reset attention before study or mock tests.

05:00

5 things you can see

Slowly look around and name five visible things.

4 things you can feel

Notice touch sensations: chair, clothes, air, or hand pressure.

3 things you can hear

Listen carefully and identify three separate sounds.

2 things you can smell

Notice two smells around you, even if faint.

1 thing you can taste

Observe one taste sensation and breathe slowly.
