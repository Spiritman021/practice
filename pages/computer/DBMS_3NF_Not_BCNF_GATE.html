Question Goal

The question asks: among four given schemas, which one is in 3NF but not in BCNF? For exam speed, check from the highest form downward: BCNF → 3NF → 2NF → 1NF.

Schema 1 is in BCNF, so it is not the answer.

3Exam Cue

A relation can be in 3NF but not BCNF only when an FD has a non-key left side and a prime attribute on the right side.
