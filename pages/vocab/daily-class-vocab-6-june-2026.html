The 10 Core Vocabulary Slides & Board Scripts

Core Vocabulary Slides

Incandescent (Adjective)

Hindi: चमकदार; उज्ज्वल; भावुक
English: Emitting light as a result of being heated. When describing individuals, it means outstandingly brilliant, passionate, or dynamic.
Synonyms: Resplendent, Effulgent, Luminous
Antonyms: Lackluster, Obscure, Dull

Abomination (Noun)

Hindi: नफरत की चीज़; घृणास्पद वस्तु
English: A thing, system, or action that causes absolute disgust, hatred, or moral loathing.
Synonyms: Detestation, Horror, Loathing
Antonyms: Blessing, Admiration, Pleasure

Prosaic (Adjective)

Hindi: साधारण; नीरस; अरुचिकर
English: Lacking poetic beauty; completely ordinary, unromantic, or boringly dull.
Synonyms: Ordinary, Dull, Mundane, Pedestrian
Antonyms: Imaginative, Poetic, New, Extraordinarily good

Personnickety / Persnickety (Adjective)

Hindi: छोटी-छोटी बातों पर अड़ने वाला; तुनकमिज़ाज
English: Placing excessive, irritating focus on trivial details; fussy or picky about precision.
Synonyms: Finicky, Fussy, Picky, Fastidious
Antonyms: Easy-going, Careless, Lax, Relaxed
Parallels: Perfectionist, Laid-back

Lusciousness (Noun)

Hindi: मधुरता; सुस्वादता; रसीलापन
English: The state of being rich, sweet, deeply appealing, or delicious to the senses.
Synonyms: Appealing, Delicious, Succulence, Opulence, Richness
Antonyms: Blandness, Insipidity, Austerity

Mendacious (Adjective)

Hindi: झूठा; कपटी; असत्यवादी
English: Habitually dishonest, untruthful, or prone to spinning false narratives.
Synonyms: False, Dishonest, Deceitful, Untruthful
Antonyms: Honest, Truthful, Sincere

Lugubrious (Adjective)

Hindi: शोककुल; उदास; विषादपूर्ण
English: Looking, sounding, or vibrating with absolute sorrow, dismal gloom, or melancholy.
Synonyms: Sad, Mournful, Melancholic, Woeful, Doleful, Gloomy, Glum
Antonyms: Jubilant, Happy, Exuberant, Elated, Euphoric, Ecstatic

Officious (Adjective)

Hindi: हस्तक्षेप करने वाला; दूसरों के काम में दखल देने वाला
English: Asserting authority or meddling in an annoying, aggressive, and uninvited way in other people's affairs.
Synonyms: Interfering, Intrusive, Meddlesome
Antonyms: Reserved, Unobtrusive, Respectful of boundaries
Label:Nosy (नाक घुसाने वाला).

Supercilious (Adjective)

Hindi: घमंडी; हेय दृष्टि वाला; अहंकारी
English: Behaving or looking as though one thinks they are superior or elite compared to others.
Synonyms: Arrogant, Haughty, Condescending, Imperious, Snooty, Hubristic
Antonyms: Humble, Modest, Down-to-earth, Unassuming

Punitive (Adjective)

Hindi: दंडात्मक; सज़ा के रूप में
English: Intended contextually as a severe disciplinary action or legal retribution.
Synonyms: Retributive, Penal, Disciplinary, Corrective
Antonyms: Rewarding, Lenient, Forgiving
