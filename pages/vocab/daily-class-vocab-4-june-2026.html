The 10 Core Vocabulary Slides

Beckons (Verb)

Hindi: बुलाना, आकर्षित करना
English: To attract or invite someone toward something.
Synonyms: Entices, Allures, Lures, Calls, Invites, Attracts, Chance
Antonyms: Repels, Deters, Discourages

Oracular (Adjective)

Hindi: रहस्यमयी, गूढ़
English: Mysterious and difficult to understand, but probably wise.
Synonyms: Esoteric, Byzantine, Arcane, Cryptic, Enigmatic, Mystical
Antonyms: Direct, Plain, Transparent, Explicit
Extensions: Sagacious, Wise, Complex

Cannibalise (Verb)

Hindi: अपने ही संसाधनों को नुकसान पहुँचाना
English: To consume, weaken, or destroy one part of something of the same kind for one's own benefit.
Synonyms: Undermine, Erode, Encroach upon
Antonyms: Strengthen, Bolster, Reinforce, Fortify
Notes: Weaken, Auto/Self

Wan (Adjective)

Hindi: पेरना, परेशान करना, कमजोर
English: Pale, weak, or sick-looking.
Synonyms: Pale, Ashen, Faint, Livid, Sickly
Antonyms: Salubrious, Healthy, Rosy, Vigorous, Animated, Lively
Extensions: Pale, Exhausted

Excoriate (Verb)

Hindi: कड़ी निंदा करना, तीखी आलोचना करना
English: To criticize someone or something very harshly and severely.
Synonyms: Denounce, Castigate, Lambast
Antonyms: Commend, Extol, Eulogize, Exalt
Extensions: Criticise, Demean

Frivolous (Adjective)

Hindi: तुच्छ, हल्का, गंभीरता से विचार न करने वाला
English: Not serious or lacking purpose.
Synonyms: Trivial, Silly, Flippant, Petty, Trifling
Antonyms: Grave, Solemn, Serious, Important, Meaningful
Extensions: Non-serious

Skirmish (Noun)

Hindi: छोटी मुठभेड़, झड़प
English: A brief and minor fight, argument, conflict, or struggle.
Synonyms: Altercation, Confrontation, Fracas
Antonyms: Harmony, Accord, Reconciliation, Concord, Agreement
Extensions: Fight

Knave (Noun)

Hindi: दुष्ट, बेईमान व्यक्ति
English: A dishonest or wicked man.
Synonyms: Impostor, Rogue, Scounddel, Villain, Miscreant, Swindler, Conman
Antonyms: Gentleman, Honest Person, Nobleman

Wanton (Adjective)

Hindi: निरर्थक, उच्छृंखल, लापरवाही
English: Deliberate and unprovoked; reckless, uncontrolled, and unjustified.
Synonyms: Unrestrained, Reckless, Random, Indiscriminate, Arbitrary
Antonyms: Deliberate, Prudent, Judicious, Rational, Sensible
Extensions: Unplanned, Excess, Uncontrolled

Pompous (Adjective)

Hindi: आडंबरपूर्ण, घमंडी
English: Self-important in an irritating and grand way.
Synonyms: Pretentious, Hubristic, Snooty, Supercilious, Haughty
Antonyms: Humble, Modest, Unassuming
Extensions: Arrogant, Snooty
