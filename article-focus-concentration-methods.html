Focus & Concentration Methods for Exam Prep

High performance in exams comes from calm attention. Short focus drills before study can improve retention and reduce careless mistakes.

Pre-Study Activation

Do 3 to 5 minutes breathing or dot tracking before starting quant practice. This settles mental noise quickly.

Attention Switching Drill

Use Number Scan and Symbol Scan for visual search speed. These drills train quick but controlled attention shifts.

Impulse Control Drill

Use Stroop Tap to improve response inhibition, useful during negative-marking tests.
