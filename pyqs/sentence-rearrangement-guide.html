🚫 Chapter 3: Words जिनसे Starting नहीं होती

Mam ने कहा — एक list बनाते जाओ!

On the other hand — दूसरी तरफ — पहले पहली तरफ होगी।

Such — ऐसा/ऐसी — पहले describe होगा।

Therefore / Hence / Thus — Conclusion। Cause पहले।

Also — भी — पहले कुछ और।

For example / For instance — Main point पहले।

However — Contrast। पहले वाली बात होगी।

It (pronoun) — पिछले noun को refer करता है।

🙋 Student: क्या It से कभी starting हो सकती है?
✅ Mam: हां! Pseudo subject में — जैसे "It is raining"। यहाँ It कोई noun नहीं refer कर रहा। लेकिन जब It किसी पिछले noun को refer करे — नहीं।

As a result — Result। Cause पहले।

⚠️ Mam की warning: "As a result = last" — ऐसा कोई fixed concept नहीं! Context देखो।

🔗 Chapter 4: Pairing Concepts
Noun → Pronoun: "Ram" वाला पहले, "he/his" वाला बाद में।
Full Form → Short Form: "United States" पहले, "US" बाद में।
Article Rule: "A man" पहले, "The man" बाद में।
Chronological: कहानी में — जो पहले हुआ वो पहले। Events को time order में।
Problem → Solution: Solution हमेशा Problem के बाद। Suggestion को ending की तरफ।
Tone Rule: पहले सारी Positive बातें → फिर However/But → सारी Negative। कभी Positive → Negative → Positive नहीं।
✶ ✶ ✶
📚 Chapter 5: Position Terminology
✅ First = Foremost / Maiden / Introductory / Beginning
✅ Last = Ultimate / Final / Terminal / Hindmost / Concluding
✅ 2nd Last = Penultimate
✅ 3rd Last = Ante-Penultimate
✅ Middle = Median
⚠️ MAIDEN (M-A-I-D-E-N) = First। MEDIAN (M-E-D-I-A-N) = Middle। Spelling में फर्क!

💡 Chapter 6: All 6 Solved Questions
Q1 — Sleep & Health
P. On the other hand, adults who sleep less than six hours are more prone to heart disease.
Q. During such long and deep sleep, the body repairs muscles and clears toxins from the brain.
R. Therefore, maintaining a regular bedtime is essential for long-term health.
S. Lack of proper sleep also weakens the immune system over time.
T. Doctors recommend that adults get seven to nine hours of rest every night.
✅ T → Q → P → S → R
T = Starting। Q — "such" pairs T's 7-9 hours। P — "On the other hand" contrast। S — "also" 2nd problem। R — "Therefore" conclusion।
Q2 — AI in Medicine
P. For example, AI can scan X-rays to find tiny fractures that humans might miss.
Q. Artificial Intelligence is now being used to help doctors diagnose diseases.
R. It can process thousands of medical records in just a few seconds.
S. However, doctors must still verify these results to ensure total accuracy.
T. As a result, patients receive treatment much faster than they did before.
✅ Q → P → R → T → S
😄 Mam:
R और P का pair बनाने वालों को महापापी award! R में seconds, T में faster — R और T का pair था।
Q3 — Great Wall of China
P. It was constructed gradually over several centuries, using materials such as stone, brick, and compacted earth.
Q. The primary reason for building such a vast and strong structure was to defend the region from invasions.
R. The Great Wall of China is one of the most well-known and iconic landmarks in the world.
S. Today, it continues to attract millions of tourists who walk along it to experience its history and enjoy scenic mountain views.
T. Extending for more than 13,000 miles, this structure stands as a remarkable example of ancient engineering.
✅ R → P → Q → T → S
Q4 — Social Media
P. Doing so helps create a positive environment for everyone on the platform.
Q. For instance, you should never post someone's private photo without asking.
R. Respecting others' privacy and opinions is the golden rule of the internet.
S. However, it is very important to follow certain rules of online behavior.
T. Social media is a great tool for staying connected with old friends.
✅ T → S → R → Q → P
Q5 — Gutenberg Printing Press

Q. This invention allowed news and knowledge to spread much faster than before.
R. And as a result, more people learned to read and stay informed about the world.
S. Johannes Gutenberg developed the movable type printing press in the 1400s.
T. Hence, it is considered one of the most influential events in human history.
✅ S → P → Q → R → T
Q6 — Olympic Games (Chronological)

🔗 Pairing Concepts & Rules

ये concepts pairs बनाने में help करते हैं।

👤 Noun → Pronoun Rule
Proper noun (Ram) वाला sentence हमेशा pronoun (he/his) वाले से पहले।
📝 Full Form → Short Form
United States → US। Full form हमेशा abbreviation से पहले।
📖 Article: a/an → the
"A man" (पहली बार) always "The man" (already known) से पहले।
📅 Chronological Order
Story passages में events को time order में। Dates देखो।
⚠️ Problem → Solution
Solution हमेशा Problem के बाद। Suggestions को ending की तरफ।
➕ "Also" Rule
"Also" = "भी" — पहले कुछ और था। Also वाला हमेशा बाद में।
↕️ Positive → Contrast → Negative
पहले positive → However/But → negative। कभी positive→negative→positive नहीं।
🔍 "Such" Pairs Back
"Such" = "ऐसी/ऐसा" — हमेशा previously described चीज़ को refer करता है।
