Key Insights for Aspirants

Pattern observations & preparation intelligence
🎯
Puzzles + Seating = 40% of Reasoning
Across all 5 years, Puzzles (10–15 Qs) and Seating Arrangement (10 Qs) consistently make up 40–50% of the Reasoning section. This is your make-or-break area.
📖
RC is English's Backbone
Reading Comprehension varies from 10 to 20 Qs (20–40% of English). Always includes vocabulary (synonyms/antonyms) within passages. Two passages are common in newer papers.
📈
Inappropriate Word = Rising Star
This topic didn't exist in 2017 but now accounts for 10 Qs (20%) in recent years (2023–2025). It's passage-based error spotting where you identify contextually wrong words.
📊
DI = 40% of Quant
Data Interpretation consistently accounts for ~20 Qs across all formats: Table, Pie, Line, Bar, and Caselet. 4 DI sets of 5 questions each is the standard pattern.
⚡
Syllogism & Inequality = Free Marks
Both appear every single year with 4–5 Qs each. With proper technique (Venn diagram / coded inequality), these are high-accuracy, low-time questions. Never skip practice.
🔄
Pattern Shift in 2025
2025 reduced from 150 to 100 Qs (only English + Reasoning). Quant was dropped. This may or may not continue — prepare all 3 subjects to be safe.

Smart Preparation Strategy

Data-backed approach to maximize your score
1
Master Reading Comprehension First
RC carries 10–20 questions every year. Read 2 editorials daily from The Hindu / Indian Express. Practice summarizing paragraphs in 1 line. Focus on vocabulary in context — synonyms & antonyms are always asked from RC passages.
2
Solve 3 Puzzles + 2 Seating Arrangements Daily
Together they form 40–50% of Reasoning. Practice Floor puzzles, Circular seating (inward/outward), Linear arrangements (2 variables), and scheduling puzzles. Start with 15-min per puzzle, target 8 mins.
3
DI = Your Quant Score Multiplier
4 sets × 5 questions = 20 marks in DI alone. Practice all formats: Table, Pie Chart, Bar Graph, Line Graph, and Caselet. Focus on calculation speed — learn percentage shortcuts, fraction-decimal conversions.
4
Nail the "Free Mark" Topics
Syllogism (5 Qs), Inequality (5 Qs), Number Series (5 Qs) appear every year and are formula/technique-based. With 2 weeks of focused practice, these become guaranteed marks. Learn Venn diagrams for Syllogism, coded inequality shortcuts.
5
Don't Ignore New Trends
Inappropriate Word (10 Qs since 2023), Column-Based Questions (5 Qs since 2023), Quantity Comparison (new in 2024) — these newer topics are gaining weightage. Practice passage-based error identification and column matching.
6
Time Management: The 80/20 Rule
Attempt high-accuracy topics first: Inequality → Syllogism → Series → Error Detection → Cloze. Then move to Seating/Puzzles (pick easier sets). Finally RC + DI. Skip 1-mark tricky questions — save time for 5-mark DI sets.
