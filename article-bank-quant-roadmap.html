Bank Exam Quantitative Aptitude Roadmap

Banking exams demand both calculation speed and calm decision-making. This roadmap builds both.

Daily Core

Do square-cube drills, multiplication speed, and reverse table conversion for 20 minutes. This improves DI and approximation speed.

Sectional Mocks

Take short sectional quant tests with time pressure and negative marking awareness. Review wrong options deeply.

Attempt Strategy

First solve direct arithmetic and simplification, then moderate puzzle-linked quant, then uncertain questions in final pass.

Mindset Routine

Use a 5-minute focus task before mocks to reduce panic clicks and rushed mistakes.
