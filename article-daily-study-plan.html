Daily Study Plan for Government Job Preparation

A realistic daily schedule is better than a perfect but unsustainable schedule. Use this 45-minute model daily.

45-Minute Core Plan

15 min table and reverse drills, 15 min speed-math practice, 15 min timed mock plus quick review.

Weekend Upgrade

On weekends, do one full-length mock and one deep analysis session. This builds exam stamina and strategy clarity.

Consistency Rule

Never skip two days in a row. Minimum fallback session: 20 minutes with one mock + one revision tool.
