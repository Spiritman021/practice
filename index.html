📖 Practice Hub Guide & README

Welcome to the official Practice Hub revision repository! This portal is built to serve as a offline-first, highly efficient revision workspace for competitive exams.

🎯 Key Features

  • Speed Math Tools: Practice multiplication tables (1-30), square roots, consecutive products, fraction/percentage conversion, and MCQ mock tests.
  • Vocab revision: Daily class summaries from lecture slides (June 2026 dates) structured with synonyms, antonyms, parts of speech, and idioms.
  • Diagnostics/Diagnostics Bar: Keeps track of errors and load status.
  • Active Reading Timer: Integrated stopwatch controls on reading pages to clock your reading speeds.

⚙️ Settings & Service Worker

The site runs a custom background Service Worker (sw.js) caching all assets for offline usage. If you lose internet connection, all math tools, vocab revision pages, and guides remain fully accessible.
