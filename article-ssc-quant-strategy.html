SSC Quant Preparation Strategy

SSC CGL and CHSL quant sections reward fast basics. Table speed and arithmetic accuracy are your compounding advantage.

Phase 1: Foundation Speed

Master tables 1 to 30, squares to 30, cubes to 20, and reverse multiplication logic. Use Table Learner and Reverse Table daily.

Phase 2: Timed Arithmetic Sets

Solve short timed sets with strict skip discipline. Avoid over-solving one hard question early in the paper.

Phase 3: Mock + Error Journal

After every mock, classify mistakes into concept, speed, and careless errors. Fix the top recurring pattern in the next session.

Weekly Pattern

4 days core speed drills, 2 days mock simulation, 1 day full revision and notebook correction.
