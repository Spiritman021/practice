Mock Analysis Framework

Your score improves fastest when analysis quality improves. Reviewing a mock properly is as important as attempting it.

Step 1: Categorize Mistakes

Mark each wrong attempt as concept, speed, or careless. This classification tells you what to fix first.

Step 2: Time Audit

List questions where you spent high time but still got wrong or skipped. These are your efficiency leaks.

Step 3: Action Plan

Convert review into next-day drills: one concept patch, one speed patch, one attention patch.
